MEET THE FOUNDER

David Hay,
Managing Director

As a residential landlord with a portfolio of properties for over 10 years, I grew frustrated that I couldn't find reliable, professional support to inspect, maintain and update my properties. After a decade of searching without success, I decided to build the solution myself — assembling a team to audit and refresh my own portfolio. The result, piloted and proven on my own properties, became RefreshP.

With a background spanning property management, interior design and furnishing, I launched RefreshP in Manchester to protect the assets of busy landlords and property sellers — ensuring the highest rental and sales returns while reducing maintenance costs through early identification of issues.

Every project follows the same principle: thorough, transparent, and delivered to a standard that genuinely moves the needle on value. The numbers speak for themselves.

David Hay
Managing Director, RefreshP

HOW WE WORK

Our process.

01

Enquire

Get in touch — by phone, email or our online form. Tell us about your property, your goals, and your timeline. We'll respond within one business day.

02

Assess

Our team visits the property and conducts a thorough room-by-room inspection. We document every item, photograph every detail, and identify every opportunity to add value.

03

Plan

We produce a prioritised schedule of works with transparent, itemised costings. Nothing proceeds until you approve. Our proposals are clear, complete, and commitment-free to receive.

04

Refresh

Our teams carry out all approved works — repairs, decoration, furnishing, dressing and photography. We coordinate every trade and discipline in-house so the process is seamless for you.

05

Reassess

The relationship doesn't end at completion. Our ongoing inspection and maintenance service ensures your property maintains its value — month after month, tenancy after tenancy.
